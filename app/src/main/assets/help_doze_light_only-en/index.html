Doze Light Only

On Android 7 (Nougat), Doze was enhanced to include a Light and Deep mode to provide more aggressive power savings. On some devices, the app may miss incoming calls only while in deep doze mode, even when whitelisted from battery saving.

Enable this option to have the app attempt to prevent your device from entering deep doze, while more agressivly applying light doze to help retain power savings improvements and maintain reliable communications.


Avoid using any other Doze app simultaniously with this setting. Eg: ForceDoze or Greenify with Agressive Doze enabled.

To use this option, you must grant the app the WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS permission.

For devices without ROOT:

  • Download and install the Google SDK tools here if you have not previously done so.
  • Enable USB DEBUGGING on your phone:
    • Open the SETTINGS app on your phone.
    • Scroll to the bottom and touch DEVELOPER OPTIONS
      • If DEVELOPER OPTIONS is not present:
        • Touch ABOUT
        • Find the BUILD NUMBER
        • Repeatedly tap on the BUILD NUMBER until you are prompted the hidden developer options has been enabled.
    • Enable the USB DEBUGGING setting
  • Connect your phone to your computer with a USB cable
  • If prompted on you Android device about the USB connection, touch ALLOW
  • On your computer:
    • Specifically for Windows
      • Click on the START button, open the COMMAND PROMPT
    • Specifically for Apple Mac or Linux
      • Open the TERMINAL
    • Type the command exactly as below, including the capitalization, then press enter:
      • adb -d shell pm grant com.meowsbox.vosp android.permission.WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS
    • After pressing enter, no message should appear. If you receive a message about no devices found:
      • Reconnect the USB cable between the Android device and computer, ALLOW the connection if prompted.
      • Your computer may need specific drivers for your Android device, see Google's help page here.
  • Disconnect then RESTART/REBOOT your Android device.
  • Congratulations you are done. The above will remain in effect until the app is uninstalled or the device is factory reset. Optionally disable the USB DEBUGGING from step 2 if desired, it is no longer needed.
